# Where Can I Send a Fax Near Me?

Finding a place to send a fax doesn't have to be difficult. Here are the most common locations where you can send faxes in most areas.

## Office Supply Stores

**FedEx Office (formerly Kinko's)**
- Available in most cities
- 24-hour locations in major areas
- Typical cost: $1.99 per page (domestic)
- International faxing available

**UPS Store**
- Wide network of locations
- Business hours vary by location
- Typical cost: $1-2 per page
- Notary services often available

**Staples**
- Self-service and full-service options
- Typical cost: $1.50-2 per page
- Copy and print services also available

## Libraries

Many public libraries offer fax services:
- Usually cheaper than commercial options
- Typical cost: $0.50-1 per page
- May have page limits
- Check your local library's website for availability

## Hotels

If you're traveling, most hotels offer fax services:
- Available at the business center or front desk
- Costs vary widely ($2-5 per page)
- Often free for hotel guests (limited pages)

## Online Fax Services

For convenience, consider online faxing:
- Send from your computer or phone
- No need to leave home
- Services like HelloFax, FaxZero, or eFax
- Some offer free trials or limited free pages

## Shipping Centers

Local shipping and postal centers often provide fax services:
- Pack & Ship stores
- Postal Annex locations
- Local print shops

## Tips for Faxing

1. **Call ahead** to confirm fax services are available
2. **Bring your document** in a ready-to-fax format
3. **Know the recipient's fax number** including area code
4. **Keep your confirmation page** as proof of transmission
5. **Consider privacy** - use cover sheets for sensitive documents

## Cost Comparison

| Location | Typical Cost | Notes |
|----------|-------------|-------|
| Library | $0.50-1/page | Cheapest option |
| UPS Store | $1-2/page | Widely available |
| FedEx Office | $1.99/page | 24-hour locations |
| Hotels | $2-5/page | Convenient for travelers |
| Online | Varies | Most convenient |

## Conclusion

The best place to send a fax near you depends on your specific needs - cost, convenience, hours of operation, and volume. For occasional faxing, office supply stores and libraries are reliable options. For frequent faxing, consider an online fax service for ultimate convenience.
